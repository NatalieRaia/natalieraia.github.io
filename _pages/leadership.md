I am committed to advancing open science, improving data infrastructure, and fostering inclusive practices in the Earth sciences through leadership and service at multiple levels.

## Extramural Leadership Positions

### AGU Board Director (2025–present)

Elected to serve on the Board of Directors of the American Geophysical Union, the world's largest Earth and space science organization. Board responsibilities include strategic governance, fiduciary oversight, and advancing AGU's mission to support and inspire a diverse community of Earth and space scientists to discover and communicate science that advances our understanding of Earth and space.

[Read the announcement →](https://fromtheprow.agu.org/benjamin-zaitchik-elected-agu-president-elect-for-2025-2026-term/)

### Associate Editor, Geoscience Data Journal (2024–present)

Serving as an Associate Editor for the *Geoscience Data Journal*, a peer-reviewed, open access journal focusing on the publication of data papers across all geoscience disciplines. Contributing to editorial decisions, peer review coordination, and advancing best practices for data publication and citation.

### Co-Chair, ESIP Physical Sample Curation Cluster (2023–present)

Leading the Earth Science Information Partners (ESIP) working group focused on physical sample curation, data standards, and persistent identifiers for geomaterials. Coordinating community engagement activities, facilitating discussions on technical and social challenges, and developing resources to improve sample data discoverability and reuse.
