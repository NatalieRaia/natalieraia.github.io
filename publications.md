## In Review

**Damerow, J., Raia, N.H., Stanley, V., Byers, N., Choe, S., Edmunds, R., et al.** (in review). Opening Doors to Physical Sample Data Discovery, Integration, and Credit. *Nature Scientific Data*.

Leveraging key use cases and significant multi-year community engagement, we outline infrastructure gaps in Earth Science sample data citation ecosystem, provide recommended best practices for sample citation, and outline key areas for future innovation.

[Preprint available →](https://doi.org/10.31223/X5ST2K)

## In Press

**Vrouwenvelder, K., Raia, N.H., Thomer, A.K.** (in press). Challenges to dataset citation using bibliographic management software. *Data Science Journal*.

We compared popular bibliographic management tools to see how they handled dataset citation import and export. The infrastructure is woefully underdeveloped to support researchers in accurately and easily citing data.

This study examines serpentinites from the New Caledonia subduction complex to understand their different tectonic origins and fluid histories during exhumation.
